Drago - How to help

 

I have been working on Drago for some years during my free time. Adding the hours I have spent on this would lead to an extravagant number. Receiving support and feedback helps to justify the time spent on it.

If you find Drago useful, if it has helped to improve, please consider one of the following ways to help.


 

Donate

The most efficient! Any amount is very welcome.

 

Link

If you own a blog or a website, adding a link to Drago will bring visitors and increase its rank in search engines. This is a simple way to help and it is very much appreciated.

 

Praise

The best advertising comes from customers. Mentioning Drago on the Go forums is a very efficient way to bring visitors.

 

Translate

 

Drago has been translated into 15 languages. It is very comfortable for users to retrieve their own language when exploring and using Drago features. This a big argument when speaking its about notoriety. Some explanations to translators are available here.

 

Test

 

There are a lot of features and settings in Drago. Unfortunately, it is almost impossible to cover all cases when producing a new version and from time to time, something may be broken. Helping to test the new versions, exploring the various settings and trying to push the program at its limits helps a lot to make it more secure. Feel free to report if you find some unexpected behavior.

 

 

Finally, thank you so much to all of you who have already helped in some way!
